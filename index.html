Owners get liquidity.

Investors get to own a picasso.

Artstock Exchange is an open market place for art owners to make an APO and offer shares of their art to investors for shared ownership and stuff.

Current art market is closed, and it needs to change.

Lack of transparency on art’s price determination and distribution channel, and on information’s basis makes the market very difficult to trust.

Perception of art being a luxury good discourages market participation and expansion.

Stock Exchange for Artworks' Shares

Create shares of an artwork as if it’s a corporation

List artwork through Art Public Offering (APO), like company stock IPOs ninjas too

An exchange platform for the open trading of art shares, which allows a transparent, accurate valuation of art that reflects the market demand

Open up the art market to be accessible to the public

A revolutionary solution, powered by state-of the-art technology

Ethereum

Using blockchain ensures data security and transparency, and Ethereum’s programmable smart contracts enable secured, 24/7 global trading.

AXOS
