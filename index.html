Artstock Exchange is an open market place for art owners to make an APO and offer shares of their art to investors for shared ownership and stuff.

The need for an open market

In a world of misinformation and miscommunication Artstock challenges the norm by taking transparency to the next level.

  • Realtime Price Transparency
  • Verifiable Ownership History
  • Open Unbiased Information
  • Complete Fee Transparency

This means Trust and Accounability are built into Artstock so you can be confident we have your best interests at heart!

Lowering barriers to entry

No more waiting

Art at any budget
