Trading Shares

What it is

Through the trading of art shares, artwork’s appropriate pricing that reflects the market’s supply and demand can be discovered. Anyone who is interested in buying an art share must submit (or take) an offer that satisfies shareholder’s demand. Any shareholder who wants to sell a share that he/she owns must submit (or take) an offer that satisfies buyer’s demand.

What it means

Investors who can properly assess the artwork’s true value can profit from the trading of art shares.
