Trading Shares

Account setup and loading currency

All users can perform both profile setup and Ethereum-based trading account setup with ease through our user-friendly system, which is more convenient than dealing with the systems of traditional financial institutions. Once the setup is complete, users can load their accounts with Digital USD (dUSD) through transfer of cash or cryptocurrency, or with a credit card purchase, to use for art share trading. Artstock Exchange also provides a unique account management system to allow users to easily manage their account balances and their trading histories.

Buying and selling art shares

A buyer who is interested in a listed artwork can always check its metadata, such as its certificate of authenticity, existence of insurance, display location, and expert reviews. Additionally, user-based reviews are also available to read or comment about the artwork. Users can study the order books for the market demands, and may place orders accordingly; any interested buyer can submit (or take) an offer that satisfies a shareholder’s demand, and any interested seller can submit (or take) an offer that satisfies a buyer’s demand. By creating a market that is similar to a stock exchange, we provide a platform already familiar to many users.

Processing the transaction

Once the order is taken and the transaction is processed, escrowed art shares and money are distributed and directly deposited to the buyer’s and the seller’s accounts, respectively. There are no blockchain gas fees involved, but 0.1% (tentatively) of the transaction amount is retrieved from both parties separately as a trading fee. Following the transaction, distributed shares can be immediately traded again, and the distributed money can be immediately cashed out. Unlike the traditional stock market where trades can only occur during open hours and require a few days to process settlements, Artstock Exchange’s 24-hour global platform offers more flexibility.
