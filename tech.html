Art Acquisition

Buying a piece outright

With the market cap valuation, a collector can offer to acquire the whole piece.

It’s as simple as making an offer for market cap + premium and escrowing that amount.

This will trigger an Acquisition Voting Period, and shareholders of that artwork can choose to sell their shares.

  • If the vote is successful, the escrowed offer amount is distributed to shareholders proportionately.
  • If the vote is unsuccessful, the full escrowed amount is returned to the offeror.
